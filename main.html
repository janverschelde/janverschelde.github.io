My research interests concern the application of polynomial homotopy continuation to scientific and engineering problems. The methodology involves the following fields: Computational Algebraic Geometry, Symbolic-Numeric Computation, Combinatorial and Polyhedral Methods, Mathematical Software and Applications, and Supercomputing.
The undergraduate courses I teach fall into three categories: basic programming (introduction, tools, and data structures), computational math (computational geometry, symbolic computation, numerical analysis), and industrial math & computation. At the graduate level I teach courses in Scientific Software and Introduction to Supercomputing.
PHCpack is a software package for Polynomial Homotopy Continuation to solve systems of polynomial equations.
